Traffic Jam 3D is set up on a busy road and you need to drive your car out of this traffic jam. Don't crash other vehicles unless you want to end the game.

Experience the driving game with realistic graphics

This game is an outstanding game of driving game world because of its attractive gameplay and 3D graphics. In this game, you have the best experience driving a car on a busy road.

Easy gameplay

In this game, your mission is to drive the car carefully on the road with hundreds of vehicles. When you drive the car, don't let your car crash the other car. Otherwise, you have to restart this driving game. You can use the arrow keys to drive the car in this game. When your car comes close to the other vehicles, let's use the left and right arrows to avoid them. Holding the up arrow key can help you speed up your car. If you want to speed down, just hold the down arrow key. On the way, you should observe the vehicles' blinkers. The other vehicles turn on the blinkers when they change lanes. Moreover, you can turn on and off the headlight continuously if you want the other vehicle to give way.

Beautiful graphics

Almost all driving games have impressive graphics. This game is designed with 3D graphics creating realistic details. You can see many different types of vehicles moving on the road and they all resemble everyday vehicles. Therefore, your car hitting them will also create dangerous accidents. In this game, you can even look through the side mirror to see vehicles going behind you. Realistic graphics and vivid sound make this game meet the needs of all players. Additionally, you can also play this game in first person or third person by pressing the C key on the keyboard.

Race mode in Traffic Jam 3D

There are 4 race mods in this game including Career, Infinite, Time Against, and Free modes. Each mode of this game can bring the most different experience to players. Now, I'm going to show you some features of these race modes.

Career mode

This mode offers many challenging levels to you. You need to drive the car to the destination without crashing the other vehicles on the road. The timer of each level is limited and you need to reach the finish line in a certain time. There are some checkpoints in the middle of the path. When your car goes through these checkpoints, some seconds are added to the timer.

Infinite and Free modes

These are two similar modes but they will still have different characteristics. These two modes do not offer a specific mission like Career mode. You just need to enjoy the feeling of driving on the road. However, you can earn some money if you overtake other vehicles on the way in Infinite mode. If you join Free mode, you will not be able to earn money. In addition, you will drive on endless roads in these two modes.

Time against

You will have a trip for a certain period of time. You need to try to travel the farthest distance within the allotted time. This mode will not add extra time to you, so you need to try to move as quickly as possible.

In short, if you are a fan of driving games, you cannot ignore this game. Please choose one of the race modes to experience this game. Hope you have a good time with this game.
